Service Item Details

Engine Oil & Filter Change

  • Toyota Landcruiser 200: Use 0W-20 synthetic oil, capacity approximately 8.5 quarts
  • Lexus LX570: Use 0W-20 synthetic oil, capacity approximately 8.5 quarts
  • Hyundai Accent: Use 5W-20 or 5W-30 oil, capacity approximately 3.8 quarts

Tire Rotation

  • Rotate tires according to the pattern specified in your owner's manual
  • Check tire pressure and adjust to recommended PSI
  • Inspect tires for wear, damage, and proper tread depth

Brake Inspection

  • Inspect brake pads, rotors, and calipers for wear and damage
  • Check brake fluid level and condition
  • Test brake system operation

Fluid Level Check

  • Engine oil
  • Transmission fluid
  • Brake fluid
  • Power steering fluid
  • Coolant
  • Windshield washer fluid

Important Notes

  • For Toyota Landcruiser 200 and Lexus LX570, synthetic oil change interval is 10,000 miles
  • For Hyundai Accent, synthetic oil change interval is 7,500 miles
  • Service intervals may need to be more frequent under severe driving conditions:
    • Extensive idling or low-speed driving
    • Driving in dusty conditions
    • Towing or carrying heavy loads
    • Frequent short trips (less than 5 miles)
    • Extreme temperatures (very hot or very cold)
  • Always refer to your vehicle's owner manual for specific recommendations
